【完整自傳 / Biography】
我出生於台灣，從小對工程與機械產生濃厚興趣。
I was born in Taiwan and have had a strong interest in engineering and machinery since childhood.

畢業後即投入工地與機械設備相關領域，累積了超過三十年的豐富經驗。
After graduation, I entered the field of construction and mechanical equipment, accumulating over 30 years of rich experience.

早期任職於漢崴與桂裕企業，參與整廠輸送與設備試車。
In the early stages of my career, I worked at Hanwei and Guey Yu, involved in full-plant conveyor and equipment testing.

後期轉任凱鴻與凱希等公司，歷任工程師、主任、經理至技術總監。
Later, I joined companies like Kai Hong and Kai Xi, holding positions from engineer to technical director.

帶領團隊完成多項國內外重大工程，包括台塑、中石化、中石油、以及台中火力電廠專案。
I led teams in completing major domestic and international projects, including those for Formosa Plastics, Sinopec, PetroChina, and Taichung Power Plant.

我擅長協調整合跨部門人力與技術資源，熟悉工程設計、現場安裝、配管與試車流程，對安全規範與施工品質有高度要求。
I excel at coordinating cross-departmental manpower and technical resources. I'm well-versed in engineering design, on-site installation, piping, and commissioning processes, with high standards for safety regulations and construction quality.

曾多次前往中國、德國、日本等地參與國際合作，累積豐富的多元文化與技術溝通經驗。
I have participated in international collaborations in China, Germany, and Japan, gaining extensive multicultural and technical communication experience.

我自認是一位敬業、細心並持續精進的工程專業人士，樂於接受挑戰與分享經驗，期許自己能繼續為台灣及國際工程界做出貢獻。
I consider myself a dedicated, meticulous, and continuously improving engineering professional. I embrace challenges and enjoy sharing my experience, hoping to continue contributing to both Taiwan’s and the global engineering field.
